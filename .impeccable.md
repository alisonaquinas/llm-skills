## Design Context

### Users
Individual developers and engineering teams/leads evaluating or installing AI agent skills for Claude Code and Codex. Primary use on desktop for installation; mobile usage is browse-and-discover (skim skills on a phone, install later at a desk). Content must be scannable at a glance on small screens.

### Brand Personality
Professional / refined. Polished and trustworthy — like a well-maintained open-source project. Subdued warmth from the brand-orange (#F46043) and cream/stone backgrounds. Never flashy; always competent.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm cream (#fff8f4) backgrounds with subtle radial brand-orange glow at the top. Plus Jakarta Sans for humanist legibility. Stone/neutral card surfaces. Brand orange used sparingly as accents. No dark-mode-first, no cyan-on-dark AI palette. Both light and dark modes supported.

### Design Principles
1. **Scannable over exhaustive** — developers skim; make hierarchy clear and copy/commands instantly visible.
2. **Subdued warmth** — brand-orange is an accent, not a background; cream tones do the heavy lifting.
3. **Competent, not clever** — refinement over decoration; every element earns its place.
4. **Mobile browse, desktop install** — mobile layout prioritizes card scanning and reading; copy-to-clipboard commands are secondary but must remain accessible.
5. **Progressive disclosure** — collapsible sections and concise defaults; detail on demand.
